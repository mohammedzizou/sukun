# Privacy Policy for Sukun

**Sukun** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection
**Sukun** is designed to work offline and respects your privacy.
- **Location Data**: We access your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is processed locally on your device and is **NOT** uploaded to any server.
- **Microphone/Audio**: We do **NOT** record audio. We only manage the device's ringer mode (Silent/Vibrate).
- **Personal Data**: We do **NOT** collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

## 2. Information Use
The information processed by the app is used solely for:
- Calculating prayer times based on your location.
- Automatically silencing your phone during prayer times.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not share any data with third-party services. The app is open-source, and you can verify the code at: [https://github.com/mohammedzizou/sukun](https://github.com/mohammedzizou/sukun)
